About Us
At Coding Ninjas, our mission is to continuously innovate the best ways to train the next generation of developers and to transform the the way tech education is delivered.
Coding Ninjas was founded in 2016 to bridge the knowledge gap between colleges and industry. Founded by Ankush Singla, Kannu Mittal and Dhawal Parate, Coding Ninjas boasts of world-class teaching faculty and a state-of-art learning platform for Coding education with faculty alumni of IIT, Stanford, IIIT and Facebook. Coding Ninjas teaches 17+ Programming courses in Foundation,l Advanced, Data & Development courses such as Machine Learning, Data Science, Web Development, Android and more. Today, Coding Ninjas ecosystem comprises of 40,000+ students and alumni, 1000+ Campus Ambassadors, 2000+ Teaching Assistants, and 150+ employees.
